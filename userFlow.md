What is AssetFlow?

AssetFlow is a software system that helps an organization keep track of all its physical assets and shared resources.

Think of it as a digital inventory and asset tracking system.

It answers questions like:

💻 Who has Laptop #123?
🪑 Which department owns these chairs?
🚗 Is the company car available?
🏢 Is Meeting Room A free from 2–3 PM?
🔧 Which assets are under maintenance?
❓ Has any asset gone missing?
What is an Asset?

An asset is anything valuable that belongs to the organization.

Examples:

💻 Laptop
🖥 Desktop
🪑 Chair
📱 Mobile Phone
🚗 Company Car
🖨 Printer
📽 Projector
What is a Shared Resource?

A shared resource is something multiple people use, but not at the same time.

Examples:

Meeting Room
Conference Hall
Company Car
Projector
Camera
Training Room

Instead of owning it permanently, employees book it for a specific time.

Real-Life Example

Imagine you're working at Google.

The company buys a new laptop.

Step 1 – Register Asset

The Asset Manager adds:

Laptop Dell XPS 15

Asset Tag: AF-0001

Category: Electronics

Condition: New

Status: Available
Step 2 – Allocate to Employee

Employee Priya joins.

The manager assigns:

Laptop AF-0001

↓

Assigned to Priya

Now the status changes to:

Allocated
Step 3 – Another Employee Wants It

Raj tries to assign the same laptop.

The system says:

❌ Already assigned to Priya.

Instead, it offers:

Request Transfer

So the laptop can't be assigned to two people at once.

Step 4 – Book a Meeting Room

Meeting Room B is free.

Rahul books it:

9:00 AM

↓

10:00 AM

Now another employee tries:

9:30 AM

↓

10:30 AM

❌ Rejected because the times overlap.

If they book:

10:00 AM

↓

11:00 AM

✅ Allowed.

Step 5 – Maintenance

Priya notices the laptop screen is broken.

She creates:

Maintenance Request

Issue

Screen Flickering

Priority

High

The Asset Manager approves it.

Now the laptop status changes:

Allocated

↓

Under Maintenance

After repair:

Under Maintenance

↓

Available
Step 6 – Return Asset

Priya leaves the company.

She returns the laptop.

Manager checks:

Screen OK
Keyboard OK
Charger Present

Then:

Status

↓

Available

Now it can be assigned to another employee.

Step 7 – Audit

Every six months the company checks all assets.

Auditor verifies:

Laptop AF-0001

✅ Found

Projector AF-0025

❌ Missing

Chair AF-0102

⚠ Damaged

The system automatically generates a discrepancy report.
